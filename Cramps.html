How To Solve Cramps


Hot water bottle,blanket, woman and female HD by @rawpixel

If you have side stiches, well then welcome to this page. From personal experience I know that side stiches suck. They hurt when you run and then they slow you down. So here are some easy tips on how to get rid of cramps.

  1. STRETCH before you go on a run
  2. Start off SLOW and build up your speed
  3. Breathe NORMAL it that doesn't work,try exhailing on your left foot
  4. If that doesn't work then try taking in a breath then waiting 3 steps before EXHAILING. Reapeat this process until the cramp is gone
  5. Use your hand to apply pressure to the painful area. I found this very HELPFUL!
  6. If you get a cramp SLOW DOWN and maybe walk to get in enough air
  7. Instead of STRETCHING before the run, stretch in the middle of the run when you get a cramp
  8. Some people may say this is bad, but when you get a cramp stop, and wait. Then, slowly start to walk again. When you inhale, put your arms over your head, so your shadow looks like a EYE. You should look like this
